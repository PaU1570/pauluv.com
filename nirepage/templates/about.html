About Me

I am Paul Uriarte Vicandi, born {{ age }} years ago in northern Spain (Basque Country). After finishing high school, I went to the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), where I obtained my bachelor's degree in physics with magna cum laude honors. Since September 2022, I am a master's student in the Quantum Engineering program at ETH Zürich.
